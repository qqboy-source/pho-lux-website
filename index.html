Our Story

In 2013, the current chef/owner of PHO-LUX launched Phở Hùng, which acclaimed food critic Alison Cook named as one of Houston's Top 100 restaurants in both 2014 and 2015.

"The moment the steaming bowl of soup lands on my table at Phở Hùng, the rest of the universe falls away from me. There's only the scent of scallion and beef broth; the delicate taste of cinnamon on my tongue; the slip and slide of long springy noodles."

In 2024, the concept of PHO-LUX was formed to showcase the chef/owner's pride in being a Houstonian, preserving traditional Vietnamese cuisine while remaining true to her passion as a car enthusiast. Whether you're a phở enthusiast or new to this flavorful dish, PHO-LUX is where vibrant, unforgettable dining experiences come together.
